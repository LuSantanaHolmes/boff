ABOUT THE FESTIVAL

The Brookly Outdoor Film Festival is a free festival that was created and pulled together by film lovers for film lovers. We carefully selected some of the best films for your to enjoy. The festival will be held in the Brookly Bridge Park and can hold up to 5000 people. Take a look at the films we have chosen for this years festival and order your free ticket.
